Welcome to City Transport Portal !

Urban citizens often face difficulties in accessing accurate and up-to-date public transport information such as route details, stop sequences, service schedules,and delay notifications. Existing systems are either fragmented across multiple platforms or lack a user-friendly interface for quick access.

This portal provides accurate and up-to-date information about public transport routes, stops, schedules, and services to help citizens travel easily.
