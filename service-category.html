Transport Service Categories

  • Regular Service (Runs daily on all major routes)
  • Express Service (Limited stops, faster travel)
  • AC Service (Air-conditioned premium buses)
  • Student Special (Operates during college hours)
  • Senior Citizen Service (Priority seating and easy access)
  • Night Service (Available after 10 PM for emergency travel)
  • Hospital Route Service (Direct connectivity to major hospitals)
